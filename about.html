About Lumina

Pioneering the future of entertainment technology since 2010.

Our Story

Lumina FX was founded with a singular vision: to create safe, spectacular, and sustainable pyrotechnic displays. We recognized a gap in the market for high-quality atmospheric effects that didn't compromise on environmental standards.

Over the years, we have grown from a small local provider to an international supplier, trusted by the biggest names in the entertainment industry. Our commitment to "Safety First" ensures that every spark is calculated and every cloud of fog is pure.
